How to use:

Notes

Keep track of what your goals during each study session is. Reflect before you start studying and record them in notes. Come back to these goals at the end of the studying session to see if you've completed them.


Timer

Note how long you've been studying for through the timer feature. Each study session is different and when you feel that you have finished or need a break, click "Finished Studying" and go take a break.


Calculator

Use to do any quick calculations


Dark Mode

Study better and more safely at night using the dark mode feature. Turn it on or off depending on what best suits your studyig habits.
